The What ,the Who ,and the Why.

Dear Friends and Animal Lovers,

We, Luis and Alma, come to you with heavy hearts, seeking your compassionate support for our beloved dog, Fluffy. Recently, our world was shaken when Fluffy was diagnosed with the parvovirus. We've been by his side in a 24-hour veterinary hospital, where he's received critical care for the past two days. The dedication of the veterinary team has been extraordinary, but the costs have escalated far beyond our means.

As a couple, we've exhausted personal resources, selling cherished items and dipping into our emergency savings to ensure Fluffy received the urgent care he desperately needed. But now, we face a crossroads. We're unable to sustain the ongoing expenses of the 24-hour care, and our only option is to seek treatment from a day-open veterinary facility. This way, we can continue his treatment during the day and care for him at home during the night.

Fluffy, who was born around November 7, 2022, isn't just a pet; he's a treasured member of our family. What's astonishing is how remarkably human-like he is in his demeanor. When we speak to him, he returns a gaze that feels remarkably understanding, almost as if he comprehends our conversations. He shares our living space, sleeping by our sides, and brightening our days with his cheerful presence. His anticipation for adventures, especially hiking, is uncanny. At the sight of our hiking shoes, he readies himself with boundless enthusiasm that warms our hearts. Even before we mention it, he's at the front door, eagerly waiting with his harness, ready for the trails.

However, this virus has robbed Fluffy of his usual vibrancy. It's heartbreaking to see him weakened, unable to eat, and requiring nourishment through a feeding tube.

Despite receiving the recommended 5-in-1 shots designed to protect against parvo, Fluffy has fallen victim to this vicious virus. It's devastating to witness his usual vibrancy diminished and his appetite gone.

Your support, no matter the amount, will make an immense difference in Fluffy's fight against this illness. If you're able to contribute even $1 or share our fundraiser with your network, it would mean the world to us. Every gesture of kindness brings us closer to bringing Fluffy back to his playful self, exploring the world with the same enthusiasm we cherish.

Thank you for considering our plea and standing by us during this challenging time.

With heartfelt gratitude,
Luis and Alma
